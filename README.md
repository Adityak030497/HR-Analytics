# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and compensation trends. The dashboard transforms raw HR data into actionable insights, helping HR teams identify retention risks and make informed workforce decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

Organizations often struggle to identify the factors driving employee attrition. This dashboard provides a centralized view of workforce metrics, enabling HR teams to:

- Monitor employee attrition and workforce health
- Identify high-risk departments and job roles
- Analyze employee demographics and satisfaction
- Support retention planning through data-driven insights

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

- **Total Employees:** 1,470
- **Active Employees:** 1,233
- **Attrition Count:** 237
- **Attrition Rate:** 16.12%
- **Average Age:** 37 Years
- **Average Monthly Salary:** 6,503

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- Employee Attrition Analysis
- Department-wise Attrition
- Job Role Attrition Rate
- Age Group & Gender Distribution
- Education Field Analysis
- Job Satisfaction Matrix
- Interactive Filters for:
  - Gender
  - Marital Status
  - Business Travel
  - Education Level

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- R&D accounted for **56.1%** of total attrition.
- Sales Representatives recorded the highest attrition rate (**39.76%**).
- Employees aged **25–44** experienced the highest attrition.
- Life Sciences had the highest attrition among education fields.
- The dashboard enables quick identification of workforce trends and retention risks.
